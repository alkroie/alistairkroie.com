Alistair Kroie

Digital Producer & UX Designer

Experience with top media brands in both Australia and the UK designing and producing content and experiences. My most recent work has been at the BBC, where I have gained a wealth of exposure to media innovations, product strategy, UX & visual design, and digital content creation.

BBC News Reactions

A recent project I have worked on as UX Designer is 'Reactions', a project taking social media embedding on BBC News to another level. In a series of prototypes we explored how a user could view popular social media posts from the BBC News website. This is currently in the prototype stage so please contact me for more details.

BBC News Social Media Style Guide

A recent design project with the BBC has been to establish the style for social media animations. A detailed style guide and tool kit for animations was developed and distributed. Key design decisions were made working within the bounds of the BBC's established style. Overview video.

Digital Product Design

BBC iWonder Audio is an example of a project where I have used skills developed during studies at General Assembly in UX design. Personas, feature prioritisation, user testing, and in-depth competitor reviews were completed to generate a product with a cohesive UI.

Interactive Strategy

In depth knowledge of media and online production has been utilised to develop an interactive strategy for Nickelodeon and Kids' Own TV. An example is Manky Manor, where a short form series was packaged by episode along with a game and extra behind the scenes content. Innovative workflows around the film production maximised use of limited resources.

Video Production

Experience producing video content for online, on-air, and mobile. Editing and motion graphic skills with After Effects & Premiere Pro are complimented with shooting skills with a variety of cameras. I also have a great deal of experience directing talent, including voice artists. Script writing, storyboarding, and pitching content have all been a big part of by career. View video work.

Virtual Reality

Alien Terrain is an experimental VR project. Available on Google's Android mobile operating system, several environments were created for users to explore, discovering information about space exploration and alien terrains.

Animation and Character Design

My role with BBC Media Action as Multimedia Producer & Animator has seen me working on a variety of different projects including a series of animated films, comic strips, and interactive graphics for iLearn, an E-Learning Product teaching people in the developing world about media production.
